Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. Covid19 is very risky tho those older people , also to out health and to those persons who are underlying medical problems . Most common symptoms:
fever

Less common symptoms:

Serious symptoms:
difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
chest pain or pressure
loss of speech or movement
